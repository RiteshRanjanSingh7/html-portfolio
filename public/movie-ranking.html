The Best Movies Of All Time According To Ritesh

My top 3 movies of all time.

Ranjhana

Movie way ahead of its time.
Romantic Love Story breaking all barriers of the society.

Jannat

Movie about the betting industry in sports.
A man enters in the world of betting just to fullfill his hunger of money.

Gadar - Ek Prem Katha

Romantic Cross-border love story between a Indian Man Tara Singh and his Pakistani wife.
It shows what lengths a man can go for his wife.
Many soulful songs and heart touching music.
